Sky Diving In Deesa



Gujarat holds the honour of being the first Indian state to have a certified drop zone for sky diving. Several skydiving tours and camps have been held in Deesa, thanks to the initiative of the Sports Authority of Gujarat. Visitors come from far and wide to experience this thrilling sport in Patan. Tourists can select from three categories of sky diving, namely static line jump, pair jump and accelerated free fall jump. Participants have to take one and a half days training before they can experience the thrilling adventure sport.
